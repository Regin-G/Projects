Onmyoji Arena

Onmyoji Arena was developed by NetEase Games and officially launched in 2018 as a mobile MOBA spin-off of the original Onmyoji RPG. It took the rich lore and characters from the Onmyoji universe and reimagined them in a competitive 5v5 battle format, blending mythological storytelling with fast-paced strategy. Since its release, the game has seen regular updates, seasonal events, and new Shikigami additions—keeping the battlefield fresh and the fanbase engaged.
