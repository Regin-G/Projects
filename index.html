Frequently asked questions

Can I bring outside food?

Small snacks and water are allowed. Larger meals must be purchased inside the park.

Are lockers available?

Yes, paid lockers are available near the entrance and water zone.

Is parking included in the ticket?

Parking is charged separately. Season Pass holders get free parking.
